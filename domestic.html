Understanding Domestic Violence

Introduction:

Welcome to our space dedicated to understanding domestic violence. At SupportCircle, we're here to provide information and support to those impacted by domestic violence in simple, real terms.

1. What is Domestic Violence? :

Domestic violence isn't just about physical abuse; it's about power and control in a relationship. It can include physical, emotional, financial, and sexual abuse, and it's never okay.

2. Types of Domestic Violence :

  • Physical Abuse: When someone hurts you physically, like hitting or punching.
  • Emotional Abuse: When someone tries to control or manipulate you with words, making you feel worthless or afraid.
  • Financial Abuse: When someone controls your money or prevents you from being independent financially.
  • Sexual Abuse: When someone forces you into sexual acts without your consent.

3. Signs of Domestic Violence :

Sometimes it's not easy to see, but signs can include bruises, physical injuries, frequent arguments, feeling scared of your partner, or feeling controlled and isolated from friends and family.

5. Breaking the Silence :

It's important to know that you're not alone and that there are people who care and want to help. Speaking up about domestic violence is the first step toward breaking free from its grip.

6. How to Get Help :

There are people and places ready to support you, whether it's a hotline, a shelter, or a counselor. You deserve to feel safe and respected in your relationships.
